In this project we demontrate the "Boid Algorithm", the algorithm that mimics the flocking behaviours of the group of organism; for example a school of fish or a flock of bird. Each particle follows three simple rules as listed below.

  • each organism try to avoid crashing into its neighbor.
  • each organism try to match its velocity with average velocity of its neighbor.
  • each organism stir slightly toward the center of the mass of the group within its vision range.

With each organism follows these simple rule, we can create an organic pattern that we may recognize in the nature. In this project, I also add more simple rules as well. I add a predator who try to catch a group of organism while each organism tries to avoid the predator. Each organism (and predator) also has speed limit in which it cannot move beyond or below the limit.
